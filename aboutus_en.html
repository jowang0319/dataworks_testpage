WHO WE Are

Our team members have a rich and varied background, including journalist from Reuters, visual arts expert from the Central Academy of Fine Arts, and computer engineer from Alibaba's. All of us proudly ensure that the four main content of datavisualization - data, technology, art and content are thourouly embeded in our projects and services.
Also, our team is a mix of expertise with over twenty years of experience as well as recent graduate from Columbia Graduate School of Journalism. In the process of mutual-learning and collision, we generate unlimited power and possibility.


Zhimin Huang

CEO of Data Works, known as "The First Person in Chinese Data Journalism", was the former CTO of Caixin Media and founder of Caixin Visualization Lab. He has won 10 international awards and 2 national ones in the area of data journalism, including SOPA awards and the nomination of the best website in data journalism.

Representative Works：
《Networking of Yongkang Zhou》
《China's Property Market Report - A Decade of Ups and Downs》
《2013 Qingdao oil pipeline explosion》
《Star Map of Nobel Prize》
《Sangong Expenditure by Units under Central Government》

Hao Chen

Co-founder of Data Works, works as professional lecturer of visual communication at Nanjing University of Arts, School of Design. During his study at China Academy of Art, he received the Jialian Wang Scholarship and was honored as outstanding graduate. His project was recognized by the China Academy of Arts.
His data designs were selected by the Twelfth National Art Exhibition of 2014 and were honored the silver award of Zijin Award Cultural Creative Design Competition. His projects were published in magazines such as Design Report from Germany.
Under his direction, his students have won many national awards as well as international ones, including the first, second and third place of the 2014 Information Design Competition of China Academy of Arts, three from a total of 6 awards given at the 2016 The Second Data Journalism Competition of Universities and the silver price of 2016 Kantar Information is Beautiful Awards, as the first prize winner from Mainland China.
