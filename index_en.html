WE DO MORE THAN DATA

"Big Data" is one of the hottest vocabulary. It seems to be everywhere. From the internet to our life, we kept in touch with this word. But what is the so-called big data? What impact does it have on us?
From this point we establish our team. We aim to materialize the abstract concept, simplify the complexity of data, and visually present it to the readers. Here, the data is no longer a string of code or beating number, it is no distant concept but a real life helper at our fingertips.

PROFOUND IN IDEA BUT EASY TO UNDERSTAND

We use data to convey information and elaborate the story, in order to change our way of thinking, to remove the tedious, and keep the essence. We would like to open up a new era of information transmission.

What is the most important element of data visualization?
